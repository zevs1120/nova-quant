# Monthly Growth Review

Last updated: 2026-03-08

This framework evaluates whether Nova Quant truly matured over the last month.

## 1) Discovery growth

Review:
- hypotheses explored,
- candidate quality trend,
- survival and promotion rates,
- regime/asset coverage expansion.

## 2) Validation quality growth

Review:
- walk-forward robustness trend,
- cost-stress survival trend,
- fragility warning trend,
- false-positive rate reduction.

## 3) Governance activity quality

Review:
- promotion/demotion quality,
- rollback responsiveness,
- retirement discipline,
- evidence completeness trend.

## 4) Portfolio intelligence growth

Review:
- diversification contribution improvements,
- drawdown concentration changes,
- family concentration risk trajectory,
- regime-stability improvements.

## 5) Product-facing opportunity quality

Review:
- opportunity contract completeness,
- actionability and clarity,
- no-trade guidance quality,
- AI explanation grounding quality.

## 6) Risk-discipline growth

Review:
- risk bucket compliance,
- size-down/no-trade correctness,
- overexposure incident count,
- discipline streak quality (without trade-frequency bias).

## 7) Monthly outcome classification

- Improved: broad-based gains with no major doctrine breaches.
- Mixed: localized improvements with unresolved weak zones.
- Deteriorated: quality drops in validation, governance, or risk discipline.

## 8) Monthly action outputs

1. top 3 architecture upgrades,
2. top 3 research priorities,
3. top 3 product translation priorities,
4. governance policy adjustments (if needed).
