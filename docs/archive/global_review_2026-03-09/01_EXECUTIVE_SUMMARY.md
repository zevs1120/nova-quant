# Nova Quant Global Review — Executive Summary

As of 2026-03-08, Nova Quant is an **early-stage AI-native quantitative decision platform** with a usable product shell and a materially upgraded research backbone.

## Mission

Nova Quant is designed to help self-directed traders reduce emotional trading and make more disciplined decisions through:

- clear daily posture,
- risk-aware opportunity filtering,
- explainable trade planning,
- and auditable research/governance logic.

It is explicitly **not** positioned as a blind prediction engine or auto-trading black box.

## Target Users

Primary users today:

- retail and small-account self-directed traders,
- users who need daily decision support and risk boundaries,
- users who value "when not to trade" guidance.

## Current Product Stage

Product stage is **usable demo + early product UX**:

- mobile-first app flow exists,
- IA has been simplified to 4 primary tabs (`Today`, `AI`, `Holdings`, `More`),
- daily check-in and discipline behavior loop are implemented,
- explainability surfaces are present.

But product runtime is still tied heavily to simulated/sample data paths.

## Current Research Stage

Research stage is **serious early platform (not production-grade execution research yet)**:

- modular strategy/regime/risk/funnel/shadow/validation/governance stack is implemented,
- discovery, evidence, portfolio simulation, and research copilot modules exist and are connected,
- contracts and docs are significantly above typical prototype quality.

Main gap: realism depth (event-level replay, live-quality fill/cost modeling, human governance workflow).

## What Nova Quant Is Trying to Become

Nova Quant is trying to become a **continuous, auditable AI-native quant research and decision platform** where:

- hypotheses become governed strategies,
- diagnostics explain low trade density and filter strictness,
- risk and regime context shape recommendations,
- and product-facing decisions are backed by evidence chains.

## Bottom-Line Assessment

- Not a toy script collection anymore.
- Not yet a production trading research stack.
- Best classification today: **credible early research platform with a strong architecture and transparent limitations**.
